What's The Data?

Web Resource

A compilation of important literature in neurosurgery, neurology and associated specialties

Welcome to the What’s the data? Web Resource, a curated collection of clinical literature pertaining to neurosurgery, neuro-oncology, spinal surgery, stroke neurology, and other related disciplines. Here you will find an organized repository of many of the higher quality studies that help to shape medical practice, along with links to relevant Pubmed entries. This site is not, and is not intended to be, comprehensive. Important studies may be excluded, and studies that are included may have been superseded by newer information. Therefore, this site should not be used in connection with the day-to-day practice of medicine, and the authors assume no responsibility for its misuse in this way. The authors hope that this resource may provide a useful framework for educators seeking to foster critical thinking and learners who desire a deeper understanding than clinical guidelines can provide.

iPhone users are invited to investigate the suite of What’s the Data? Apps available on Apple App Store, which provide helpful summaries of these studies in a quick and easy-to-read format.
